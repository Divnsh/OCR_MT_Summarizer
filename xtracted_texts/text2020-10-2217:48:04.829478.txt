   

Original text in Spanish

  
 

A principios de Noviembre queda oficialmente inaugurada la nueva
temporada de pesca deportiva que incluye toda la jurisdiccién de rios
y lagos de la provincia del Neuquén. De ésta manera la apertura de la
temporada en Neuquén se lleo a cabo dos dias antes con respecto
del resto de la Patagonia.

 
       
   
 

Esta temporada que se inicia presenta importantes modificaciones en
el reglamento de pesca deportiva, que privilegia la devolucion
obligatoria de las piezas vivas al agua y la actividad con anzuelos sin
rebaba .

      
   
     
 

English version on website

In early November it is officially opened the new season of sport
fishing jurisdiction that includes all rivers and lakes in the province of
Neuquén. In this way the opening of the season in Neuqueén Ileé out
two days before with respect to the rest of Patagonia.

     
 
 
  

This season starts has significant changes in the rules of sport fishing,
which favors mandatory return of living water and activity with
barbless hooks pieces.

  
 
   
   

Improved English version

  

The fishing season for all rivers and lakes in the Province of Neuquén
starts at the beginning of November, two days before other regions
of Patagonia.

This year the regulations for recreational fishing have changed. Only
barbless hooks may be used and all fish caught must be returned to
the water.
